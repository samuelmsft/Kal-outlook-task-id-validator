Active: This add-in will check your email subjects before sending.

Required Format:

[Task_ID - Project_Name] Email subject

Valid Examples:

  • [2532 - FDA] Create service account

How it works:

When you click Send, the add-in automatically checks if your subject line includes a task ID in the correct format. If it's missing, you'll see a warning and the email won't be sent until you add it.
